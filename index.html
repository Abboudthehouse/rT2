About AgentAccess

AgentAccess is designed to provide agents with valuable information and tools to better support their clients — YOU. It’s built to keep clients safe, informed, and empowered by giving more options and transparent resources for the home buying and selling journey.

What is Self Tour?

A self tour lets a buyer visit a home on their own schedule — without their agent physically present — but still under agent supervision and approval. This gives clients freedom to explore homes at their convenience, while agents keep control of access, approvals, and safety protocols.

What is AgentAccess?

AgentAccess is your all-in-one digital assistant for managing showings. Agents can approve buyer self-tours, track legal waivers, coordinate communication, and maintain full control — while clients gain flexibility and safety.
